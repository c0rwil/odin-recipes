Horchata

a great choice for any occasion

Ingredients list:

  • 1 cup of white rice
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup chopped almonds
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tbsp vanilla
  • 1 12 oz can of Evaporated Milk
  • 1.5 cups of almond milk
  • 1 liter of water
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  • Soak rice, cinnamon, almonds in a bowl of water overnight or at least 5 hours for rice to slightly soften
  • Strain water from the mixture, dispose of the water
  • Blend the concoction with evaporated milk until grains of rice are completely ground
  • Strain resulting liquid into a pitcher, add the sugar, vanilla, and milk.
  • Mix with 1 liter of water and service with some ice

Agua de Jamaica

a wonderfully simple & refreshing drink

Ingredients list:

  • 2 cup of Hibiscus flowers
  • 2 cup of Sugar
  • 1 pitcher of Cold Water
  • Ice cubes
  • (Optional) 2 Lemon Slices

Instructions:

  • Place hibiscus flower into saucepan
  • Fill saucepan to top with water
  • Bring water to boil for ~10 minutes or until water is a deep red color
  • Steep for ~1 hour
  • Strain contents
  • Add sugar
  • Pour into large pitcher
  • Add cold water until about 5 shades lighter (vibrant purple/red)
